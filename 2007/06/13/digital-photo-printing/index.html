Digital photo printing

13 Jun 2007

I recently decided I wanted to try to find a clean way to make some nice prints from pictures I’d taken.

A friend of mine from work pointed out White House Custom Color. They have a somewhat arduous process for signing up with them, but its well worth it. You also, as part of this process, submit to them 5 images you want printed as “test print”. They ship them back to you as 8×10′s. I received mine about 36 hours after signing up, so there time-to-return on work appears to be very quick. The prints were exceptional. I was very impressed.
