I should have realized…

14 Feb 2008

In the World of Warcraft, they have these non-combat pets that are cute and that obediently follow you wherever you may roam. I’ve been addicted to getting them for some time, even some of the harder to obtain ones, like Mr.Pinchy.

It should have stood to reason that playing the Hunter class (and having combat pets), that I would immediately immerse myself in getting the harder to obtain pets in the game. So, this is the beginning of that odyssey.

First one: Humar the Pridelord. He’s a black lion that sits under a nice shady spot in the Barrens, just north of Ratchet. As soon as I get my screenies processed, I’ll throw one into this post. God, how cool. I’m such a nerd.
