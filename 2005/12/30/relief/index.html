Relief

30 Dec 2005

Relief from the long hours and stresses of work appears to be on the way, both in terms of manpower and hours.

We have two new people on our team now, one who is the other Shift Manager opposite me, which is an immediate drop by half of the workload. The other is the TDO, who is backfilling the position I am vacating, and as soon as he’s trained, I can more officially move into my Shift Manager role.

Finally, we’re also going to be shifting the work days to be a bit more conducive to a night schedule and to make it more fair for everyone. This will have the side effect of also making sure that the minimum time off after a shift week is 4 days. Amen, Hallelujah and Praise Be. I can’t wait.
