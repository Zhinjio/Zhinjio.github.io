Let the cleansing begin…

14 May 2005

So my wife’s Mom is coming to visit later today, and staying for a week. This sort of thing always is preceeded by a tremendous effort to clean the house and make things reasonably presentable. Try to mix that in with trying to get enough sleep and still spending time with the other usual weekly activities, and you invariably fall short on something. In this case, sleep.

But that aside, I’m looking forward to it. She’s always an easy guest to please, and enjoys the relaxation of not being at home and having to work, etc. I believe they’re also going to visit some Indian thing next week mid-week. I’d link it, but I haven’t a clue what it is. I’ve only listened with passing interest. I do know its like … 5+ hours away or something, near the four corners. Anasazi maybe? Eh, whatever, I’ll be working anyway. Oh, I added the ‘Reading List’ and ‘Shopping List’ pages to the right hand menu there. We’ll see how I like them and if I keep them up to date or not. A grand experiment.
