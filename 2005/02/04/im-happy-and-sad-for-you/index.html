I wanted to try and introduce the Style Switcher into my site to give people some options on how to display things. I realized fairly quickly that, at least in the case of that particular style switcher mod, that I would have needed to have introduced that concept before I started making significant changes to the index.php file or css file to achieve the current effect.

Thinking about it a little bit more, I believe the manner in which I layout the site and present you with all of this blather is my choice, and is, in fact, a part of the whole of the delivery of the content, for whatever that is worth. So is that egotistical and self-centered and wanting to show up my m@d sk177z with php and css? Not so much. It just means I was trying to achieve a particular look and feel, and once I found it, I wanted the site to be viewed that way. If you don’t like it, hit me up for the RSS feed and read it in your aggregator and leave me the hell alone.
