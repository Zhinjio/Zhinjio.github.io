preparation

13 Oct 2009

Its that time of the year again.

Its time to start wondering if I’m up to the challenge of nanowrimo again or not. I read somewhere that it takes 20 days to formulate a plot, theme, and main protagonists enough to fully flesh out a novel in the timespan nanowrimo allows.

So I’m 3 days behind. Fortunately, the thoughts already percolating are reasonable this year, and I think I may actually have some juice to squeeze out. Time, as always, is the biggest challenge. But I think if I’m careful, I can get that as well.

So finally, there is the “habit” of writing something every day. So get ready for 17 days of some pretty banal writing on here in preparation. Wish me luck.
